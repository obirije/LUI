LUI replaces your Android home screen with a conversational AI assistant that actually controls your phone. No icons, no widgets — just speak or type what you want and it happens.

106 native device tools work instantly through voice or text. Say "turn on the flashlight" and it turns on. Say "call mum" and the call places. Say "navigate to the nearest pharmacy" and Maps opens with directions.

Features:

- Voice-first with wake word ("Hey LUI"), conversation mode, natural TTS
- Cloud LLM: Gemini, Gemma, Claude, OpenAI, or Ollama with native function calling
- Health ring support — pair a Colmi R09 for heart rate, SpO2, HRV, stress, sleep, steps, and temperature tracking
- Wellness mode — ambient sounds, notification filtering, and screen dimming to manage stress
- Proactive stress alerts when your ring detects sustained high stress
- Web search and browse any URL — results shown as clickable cards
- Camera and gallery with AI image analysis
- Geofence and scheduled triggers
- Notification triage with 2FA code extraction
- Screen pilot — read and interact with any app via voice
- 28 app deep links: Spotify, YouTube, Netflix, and more

Connect your AI agents:

LUI exposes all 106 tools over an MCP-compatible WebSocket bridge. Connect Claude Code, OpenClaw, Hermes, or any agent framework. Your agents get a physical phone body.

pip install lui-bridge
npm install -g lui-bridge

All data stays on your phone. Open source. GPLv3.
